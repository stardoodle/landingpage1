Rocket Propulsion — Introduction
By Prof. Saptarshi Kolay  ·  Week I, Lesson 1  ·  31 mins  ·  1,240 views
About this lesson
In this lesson, we explore the fundamentals of rocket propulsion — from Newton's third law to understanding how engines are designed to generate thrust in the vacuum of space. We'll cover fuel and oxidizer systems, combustion chamber mechanics, and how nozzle design affects rocket performance. This lesson builds the foundation for understanding how spacecraft escape Earth's gravity and travel through space. ...more
We'll also touch on Kepler's laws of planetary motion, the history of human spaceflight, and an overview of major space agencies and private players shaping the future of aerospace. By the end of this lesson, you'll have a clear picture of the landscape of modern space exploration.
